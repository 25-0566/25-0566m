AUTOMOTIVE Heritage

1950s
Post-War Renaissance

The Birth of the Sports Car

After World War II, European manufacturers turned aircraft engineering into automotive art. Jaguar launched the XK120, Mercedes revealed the 300SL Gullwing, and Ferrari began its legendary racing dynasty.

🏁
1960s
The Golden Age

Style, Speed, and Revolution

The swinging sixties brought us the Jaguar E-Type—called "the most beautiful car ever made" by Enzo Ferrari. The Porsche 911 debuted and American muscle emerged with the Chevrolet Impala defining cool.

🎸
1970s
Muscle & Motorsport

Raw Power Meets Racing Pedigree

The 1970s gave us the golden era of American muscle—the Chevrolet Chevelle SS with its legendary 454 LS6 engine producing 450 horsepower. Ferrari refined its V8 engines, and BMW's motorsport division was born.

💪
1980s
German Precision

Engineering Excellence

The 1980s saw German manufacturers reach new heights. The BMW M3 E30 became the most successful touring car ever. The Porsche 911 Turbo defined the supercar era. Mercedes perfected grand touring with the 500SL.

🇩🇪
1990s
Refined Luxury

The Modern Classic Emerges

The 1990s bridged analog driving with modern reliability. The Lexus LS400 shocked the world. The Range Rover Classic defined luxury SUVs. The Jaguar XJS and BMW 635CSi offered grand touring comfort.

ABOUT LUX DEALERSHIP

Our Story

Founded in 2014 by Rudolph Maina, LUX DEALERSHIP began with a singular vision: to preserve and celebrate the golden era of automotive excellence.

What started as a passionate restoration project in a modest Westlands garage has grown into one of East Africa's most trusted classic car dealerships.

Today, LUX DEALERSHIP stands as a testament to timeless design, offering meticulously curated vehicles that tell stories of innovation, luxury, and the open road.

"Every classic car has a soul. Our job is to help it find its next chapter."
