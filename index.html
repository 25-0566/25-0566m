ABOUT LUX DEALERSHIP

Our Story

Founded in 2014 by Rudolph Maina, LUX DEALERSHIP began with a singular vision: to preserve and celebrate the golden era of automotive excellence.

What started as a passionate restoration project in a modest Westlands garage has grown into one of East Africa's most trusted classic car dealerships.

Today, LUX DEALERSHIP stands as a testament to timeless design, offering meticulously curated vehicles that tell stories of innovation, luxury, and the open road.

"Every classic car has a soul. Our job is to help it find its next chapter."
— Rudolph Maina, Founder
